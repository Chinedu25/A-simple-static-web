About

[i]The Lost Secret of the ancients[/i] by Chris Reynolds is about the Adventure of Dr. Maya Harrington on a quest to find her missing father as she unravels secrets left behind by ancient master builders across different regions world from archeological sites in Egypt to the stone Hedge in England revealing the secrets left by these master builders utilizing the resources of a billionaire who has an interest in her capabilities. Maya will embark on her Adventure with her close friends, Johnny White Feather (JW) and Layla as they help her with their unique set of skills as they decrypt these ancient mysteries but a secret organization was bent on towards finding Maya’s Father, Alex Harrington for he was a threat to this organization that has existed in shadows for millennia and he had abilities to sabotage them so they plan on using Maya and her friends to get him. Throughout the book, Maya gains new Knowledge, and her world view changes, the readers perspective on various concepts referenced in the book could also change.
